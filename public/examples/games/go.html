Quick Rules

  • Click on intersections to place stones
  • Surround opponent's stones to capture them
  • Stones with no liberties (empty adjacent points) are captured
  • Ko rule: Cannot immediately recapture (simplified)
  • Pass your turn if you have no good moves
  • Game ends when both players pass consecutively
